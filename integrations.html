Say goodbye to endless price sheets

StoreCat’s team of engineers is here to help you automate without your own technical team. StoreCat connects to each of your suppliers and automatically pulls new item information from your suppliers whenever it's available.
This information includes pricing, new items, and product details. If there’s something else you need, StoreCat will be sure to grab that too. Learn More about Connecting With Suppliers

Modernize without Reinventing

Most management systems are not E-Commerce oriented - StoreCat was made for the web. We can help you bridge your sales team to your supply chain, and give you the room you need to store all of your information in one place.
Keep your product catalogue organized and up-to-date, with all the numbers you need in one place. With automatic updates, your team will always be able to buy at the best price, and easily purchase and manage your inventory.
